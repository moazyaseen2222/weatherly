
*** Weahter Wave ***
Nicely wather app with beutiful UI.
- Features:
 1- Search your city and get the current weather.
 2- With your city weather you will got the other weahter inforamtion like:
    - Temp & main status.
    - Description about weahter.
    - Max & Min Temp.
    - Humidity &  Pressure & Wind Speed
 3- Favorite List :
      - Save your favorite cites list and get thire weahter faster.
 4- Stopwatch counter:
      - You can start the Stopwatch and count the time.
 5- Timer counter:
      - You can set the time and use timer to count down.
